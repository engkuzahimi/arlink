About Us

Company Background

ARLink offers interactive business cards and seasonal greeting cards straight to our customers hands. These cards offer a familiar approach of the conventional cards yet is more powerful with the extension of Argumented Reality (AR) features embedded which brings a more engaging and informative information to the users.

Our Management

Muhammad Husaini Bin Mohd Sabri

Chief Executive Officer

Muhammad Husaini acts as the Chief Executive Officer will oversee the company’s overall vision and mission of ARLink and ensures the product made is up to the company’s quality and expectations. Husaini will also be leading the other important high-level position and evaluate the departmental performance.

Nur Hanisah Mohd Fuzi

Chief Financial Officer

Hanisah as the company’s CFO is considered as the highest-level finance controller for the whole company. She is the one that will manage the company’s income and expenses. She also leads the company’s financial team and provide recommendations directly to the CEO. Hanisah will take part in managing financial forecasting and budgeting processes and advise long-term business opportunities that will benefit the company in the long run.

Engku Muhammad Zahimi Bin Che Engku Ibrahim

Chief Technology Officer

Zahimi is the Chief Technology Officer for the company and is reporting directly to the CEO. His job scope is to come up with policies and procedures in the development of the projects and products. Zahimi is also responsible on coming up with latest best techniques to develop products through constant research and development (R&D).

Mohd Adib Fikri bin Kamar

Chief Marketing Officer

As the company’s CMO, Adib Fikri will be reporting directly to the CEO about the marketing affairs in the company. Adib Fikri is knowledgeable and can be agile to understand the fast-paced market conditions and formulate plans for the company to adapt with the situation. He will be the mastermind of marketing and advertising initiatives for the company.
